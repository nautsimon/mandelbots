Who are we?

We are a rookie team based in Urbana Maryland. Our team consists of 12 high schoolers who are all extremely interested in robotics and other STEM fields. Our team name is The Mandelbots and we look forward to see you at qualifiers!

Why the Mandelbots?

The Mandelbots are named after the Mandelbrot set, a series of complex numbers, that when plotted illustrates the beauty of mathematics through a mysterious pattern.
